Sample Superstore Report

The 'Sample Superstore Report' is a comprehensive analysis of sales data taken from the Kaggle platform. The data was first cleaned and transformed using PowerQuery, before being analyzed in PowerBI. The report provides valuable insights into the sales performance of the superstore, including the total sales of $13 million and total profit of $1.47 million. One of the key highlights of the report is the line graph showing the profit by year and quarter from 2012 to 2015. This visualization helps to demonstrate the overall trend in profit over time and provides a quick and easy way to see the fluctuations in profit by quarter. In addition to the line graph, the report also includes a bar graph showing the profit by sub-category. This allows for a more detailed analysis of profit by product type, providing valuable insights into which sub-categories are performing the best. Overall, the 'Sample Superstore Report' is a powerful tool for understanding sales performance and uncovering insights into the data. It provides a clear and concise representation of the data, making it easy to understand and use for business decision-making."
